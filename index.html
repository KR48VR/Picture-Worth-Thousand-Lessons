      Teacher guide

      Suggested lesson flow

      5 min — Notice

      Show the original photo first. Ask students what science or maths they can see before revealing the annotated poster.

      10 min — Explain

      Use the Secondary tab for a whole-class concept map, or the University tab for deeper modeling and equations.

      15 min — Transfer

      Move to Nature → Engineering. Ask: what stayed the same, and what did engineers add?

      Extension

      Students design a new technology using one principle from a different animal photo.
