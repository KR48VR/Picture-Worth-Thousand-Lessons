      Suggested lesson flow

      5 min — Notice

      Show the original photo and ask what is happening before revealing the annotated visual.

      10 min — Explain

      Compare the original and the selected explainer side by side, and identify which ideas became visible.

      10 min — Transfer

      Use the Nature → Engineering mode to discuss how natural principles are translated into design.

      Extension

      Invite students to build their own mini module from a new wildlife image using the same structure.
